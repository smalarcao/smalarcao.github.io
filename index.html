About Me:

I am an Assistant Professor at the Informatics Department at the Faculty of Sciences, University of Lisbon (DI/FCUL) since 2024. I was an Invited Assistant Professor from 2022 to 2024 at DI/FCUL, and a Teaching Assistant at Instituto Superior Técnico (IST) from 2016 to 2022. I have a Ph.D. (2022) in Informatics (DI/FCUL) and a BSc (2013) and MSc (2014) in Information Systems and Computer Engineering (IST). I am an Integrated Researcher at LASIGE and member of the Tech and People Lab. My research focuses on developing assistive technologies to deliver personalized and emotion-aware interventions for people living with cognitive impairments and autistic individuals. I authored more than twenty peer-reviewed research publications at international conferences and journals, and participated in multiple EU-funded research consortia as a hired researcher/member (e.g., COST action, Horizon 2020, FP7-SPACE). I am also a member of the ACM and ISTAART - Alzheimer's Association.
